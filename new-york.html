BROOKLYN BRIDGE

Exclusive architectural design.

Brief Description ▼

Completed in 1883, the Brooklyn Bridge is an iconic, neo-Gothic suspension bridge connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn. Renowned for its stone towers and steel cables, it offers panoramic skyline views for pedestrians, cyclists, and motorists. It is considered a must-visit, historic landmark featuring a safe, elevated walkway.

Location Westminster, London
Summer Tours July 9 – Sept 27
Heritage Established 1703

Visitor Experience

Stunning Views: Visitors frequently describe the views of the Manhattan skyline as "magnificent" and "iconic," especially during sunset. Crowd Management: It can get very crowded, so early morning is recommended to avoid heavy foot traffic and vendors.
