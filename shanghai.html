The Rise of Shanghai’s Skyline
01 / 05

SHANGHAI TOWER

The tallest building in China.

SHANGHAI SKYLINE

A futuristic blend of innovation and design.

LUJIAZUI DISTRICT

The financial heart of modern Shanghai.

Brief Description ▼

The Shanghai Tower is China’s tallest skyscraper and the world’s second tallest building, standing at 632 meters in the Lujiazui financial district. Completed in 2015, it is a marvel of modern engineering and sustainability, featuring a unique twisting design and sky-high observation decks.

Location Lujiazui, Shanghai, China
Visiting Hours Daily 09:00 – 21:00
Completed 2015

Visitor Experience

Observation Decks: Visitors can enjoy 360-degree views of Shanghai from multiple high floors.
Fastest Elevator: Reach the top in just 55 seconds with one of the world’s fastest lifts.
Modern Architecture: Experience the tower’s twisting design and sustainable features.
Night Views: The city lights from above create an unforgettable experience.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION
+

Shanghai Tower

Standing tall in the heart of Shanghai’s financial district, the Shanghai Tower combines elegance and cutting-edge engineering. Visitors can ascend to its sky-high observation decks, marvel at the twisting façade, and enjoy one of the most spectacular city views in the world.

The Iconic Pearl of Shanghai
01 / 05

ORIENTAL PEARL TOWER

A futuristic symbol of Shanghai’s skyline.

SHANGHAI AT NIGHT

A dazzling city illuminated with modern brilliance.

LUJIAZUI SKYLINE

The heart of Shanghai’s financial district.

Brief Description ▼

The Oriental Pearl TV Tower is Shanghai’s iconic landmark located in Pudong. Completed in 1994, the tower stands at 468 meters and is famous for its distinctive spheres, futuristic design, and panoramic observation decks overlooking the Huangpu River.

Location Pudong, Shanghai, China
Visiting Hours Daily 08:00 – 21:00
Completed 1994

Visitor Experience

Observation Spheres: Experience multiple levels of panoramic views of Shanghai.
Transparent Skywalk: Dare to walk on the glass-bottom skywalk for a thrilling view.
History & Culture: The tower includes a small museum explaining its construction and significance.
Night Illumination: The tower lights up beautifully after dark, creating stunning photo opportunities.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION
+

Oriental Pearl TV Tower

With its futuristic spheres and towering height, the Oriental Pearl TV Tower is an emblem of modern Shanghai. Visitors can explore observation decks, walk the glass skywalk, and enjoy sweeping views of the Huangpu River and city skyline.

The Tranquil Beauty of Yu Garden
01 / 05

YU GARDEN

A masterpiece of classical Chinese landscape design.

TRADITIONAL PAVILIONS

Elegant structures reflecting ancient Chinese artistry.

SERENE LANDSCAPES

A peaceful retreat in the heart of Shanghai.

Brief Description ▼

Yu Garden is a classical Chinese garden located in Shanghai’s old city, dating back to the Ming Dynasty. Known for its exquisite landscaping, traditional pavilions, rockeries, ponds, and stone bridges, it provides visitors a serene escape from the bustling city while showcasing China’s ancient garden design and cultural heritage.

Location Huangpu District, Shanghai, China
Visiting Hours Daily 08:30 – 16:45
Established 1559 (Ming Dynasty)

Visitor Experience

Scenic Walks: Meander through pavilions, rockeries, and tranquil ponds.
Historical Architecture: Admire traditional Ming-era design and craftsmanship.
Cultural Insight: Learn about classical Chinese gardening and heritage.
Photography & Relaxation: Perfect spot for serene photos and quiet reflection away from the city hustle.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION
+

Yu Garden

Yu Garden is a masterpiece of classical Chinese landscaping, offering visitors a peaceful retreat into history. With winding paths, ornate pavilions, and koi-filled ponds, it captures the elegance of Ming Dynasty garden artistry.

The Timeless Elegance of The Bund
01 / 05

THE BUND

A historic waterfront along the Huangpu River.

SHANGHAI NIGHT SKYLINE

A dazzling fusion of history and modernity.

HUANGPU RIVER

The lifeline flowing through Shanghai’s heart.

Brief Description ▼

The Bund is Shanghai’s historic waterfront area along the western bank of the Huangpu River. Famous for its stunning collection of colonial-era buildings and panoramic river views, it represents the fusion of East and West in Shanghai’s architectural heritage. Visitors can stroll along the promenade and enjoy the skyline of modern Pudong across the river.

Location Huangpu District, Shanghai, China
Visiting Hours Daily 24 Hours
Established Late 19th Century

Visitor Experience

Riverside Walks: Enjoy scenic strolls along the promenade with the skyline in the background.
Architectural Tour: Marvel at historic colonial-era buildings with European-inspired designs.
Night Views: The Bund lights up at night, offering spectacular photo opportunities.
Cruises & Dining: Optional river cruises and riverside restaurants provide a full Shanghai experience.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION
+

The Bund

The Bund is a vibrant historic district where Shanghai’s past meets the present. Visitors can explore iconic colonial buildings, enjoy panoramic river views, and experience the city’s lively waterfront atmosphere.

The Heights of Modern Shanghai
01 / 05

SHANGHAI WORLD FINANCIAL CENTER

A global icon of finance and architectural excellence.

SHANGHAI SKYLINE

A breathtaking display of futuristic skyscrapers.

LUJIAZUI DISTRICT

The financial core of Shanghai’s modern economy.

Brief Description ▼

The Shanghai World Financial Center (SWFC) is a supertall skyscraper located in the Lujiazui area of Pudong. Completed in 2008, it stands 492 meters tall and is known for its distinctive trapezoid aperture at the top. The tower combines modern architecture with world-class observation decks, offices, hotels, and retail spaces.

Location Lujiazui, Shanghai, China
Visiting Hours Daily 09:00 – 21:00
Completed 2008

Visitor Experience

Observation Decks: Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of Shanghai from the top floors.
Glass Skywalk: Dare to walk on the transparent sky deck for thrilling views.
Modern Architecture: Marvel at the iconic trapezoid aperture and innovative design.
Hotels & Shopping: The tower includes luxury hotels and shopping areas, making it a full experience destination.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION
+

Shanghai World Financial Center

Shanghai World Financial Center is a symbol of modern Shanghai, blending architectural elegance with cutting-edge engineering. Visitors can explore its observation decks, enjoy breathtaking city views, and experience one of the most iconic skyscrapers in the world.
